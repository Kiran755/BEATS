About Us

DJS Beats is the official music club of Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering, Mumbai. This is an initiative taken by music loving DJSCE students with the goal of spreading the love for music and promoting local talent by giving them a platform to showcase their unique talent and skills.
